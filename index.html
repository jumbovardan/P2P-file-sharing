What is P2P Share

We are a free and independent peer-to-peer (P2P) file sharing service from India that prioritizes your privacy and keeps your data safe. We store nothing online: simply close your browser to stop sending.

Our mission is to make sure people keep their data safely into their own hands, as it should be.
